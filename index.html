Intro

Welcome to my portfolio! As you might of guessed, I am a Data Analyst. I began this journey at a very young age when I worked with my grandma in a candy store. I would help her with the stock and computer software she was using. During the time of Windows 3.1 and DOS. This built my foundation in analytics and a life long love for business. I hope you are excited for the future and what we can build together with data! Check out a few of my projects work.
